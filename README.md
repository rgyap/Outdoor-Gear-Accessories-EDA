# Outdoor-Gear-Accessories-EDA
Exploratory data analysis based on an assignment from De La Salle University Manila

## The Data

The data set contains information related to sales of outdoor gear, particularly the following:
- `Date`, the date of transaction (`object`),
- `Year`, the year when the transaction took place (`float64`),
- `Month`, the month when the transaction took place (`object`),
- `Customer Age`, the age of the customer (`float64`),
- `Customer Gender`, the gender of the customer (`object`),
- `Country`, the country where the transaction was made (`object`),
- `Product Category`, the type of outdoor gear that was sold (`object`),
- `Subcategory`, the more specific type of outdoor gear that was sold (`object`),
- `Quantity`, the quantity of the sold item (`float64`),
- `Unit Cost`, the cost of buying an item for the business (`float64`),
- `Unit Price`, how much an item is being sold for (`float64`),
- `Cost`, `Quantity` times `Unit Cost` (`float64`),
- `Revenue`, `Quantity` times `Unit Price` (`float64`).

## Objectives of the EDA

The goal of the EDA is to provide answers to the following questions:

1. Which U.S. state is best for one to locate an (outdoor gear) accessory business?
2. What are the most profitable accessory products?
3. Which demographic of customers (based on age or gender) is most likely to buy accessories?
